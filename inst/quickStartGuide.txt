
0. Start R

1. Load the Package (rJava has to been installed):

		> library(RNetLogo)

2. Open NetLogo session:
		> NLStart("C:/Program Files/NetLogo 4.1.2", nl.version=4)

3. Load a model
		> NLLoadModel("C:/Program Files/NetLogo 4.1.2/models/Sample Models/Earth Science/Fire.nlogo")

4. Submit a command:
		> NLCommand("setup")
		> NLDoCommand(10, "go")

5. Get a value:
		> br.trees <- NLReport("burned-trees")
		> print(br.trees)



See the manual pages and the tutorial for deeper insights.
